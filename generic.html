Project 2
Chatbot

Aayush Shrestha, Anson Chan Yin Wan, Benedict Casino, Endrave Wong, Wesley Hui, Zachary Guard

We have designed a chatbot to assist users during their grocery trips

Background

Not all people go to supermarkets/wet markets on a daily basis, and getting around inside a market while comparing prices between differnt stores could be a challenging task, so we decided to design a chatbot to help these type of people to save time and effort.

Ideate

We have identified several needs for common market-goers, namely keeping track of what to buy, price comparison, coming up with ideas to cook, locating products in the market.
