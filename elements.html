Project 3
VeRse

Aayush Shrestha, Anson Chan Yin Wan, Benedict Casino, Endrave Wong, Wesley Hui, Zachary Guard

We have designed a virtual marketspace for people to shop online.

Background

With Covid19 still around the world, shopping online is the goto way of shopping for a lot of people, but studies show that there is still a charm about shopping in the traditional, face to face way, so in an effort to mimic the ofline shopping experience, we designed VeRse, a online virtual marketspace.

  • Full Story

Ideate

Currently online stores allows customers to view all type of products, however, they cannot interact with the product directly without buying it first. Online shopping also has very little social interaction since most of the time customer are shopping alone and cannot contact the seller immediately.

Prototype

Script for the video

Our prototype visualizes the experience of VeRse where customers can walk around (with or without an VR headset) in a virtual 3D marketspace where they can go around just like in a normal market/mall to check out different brand and their products in minibooths, from which they can go into the portal of that brand to go to the virtual store for that brand to view their products. Customers can also shop with their friends together in VeRse as well as talk to sales/managers of stores while they are shopping. Previous projects (Handler, Mart-E) can also be implemented into this virtual market to enhance the experience. Finally, customers can also enjoy non-physical services in VeRse like watching movies.

This project is an interesting experience since we are trying to recreate a physical market into an VR environment, the process of building the scene in Spoke was very fun and it is exciting to see that our previous projects can also have a place in this virtal market, it felt like everything is coming together.
