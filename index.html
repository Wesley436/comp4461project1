Ideate

The basic function of our project is to translate sign language to texts in real time. There were a few early ideas that were suggested such as a mobile application (but it is difficult for people to hold a phone and do sign language at the same time), a plugin for existing communication platforms (but we need to strike a deal with them), using LEAP motion / special gloves to capture hand movements (but it is quite costly), finally we choosed to design a standalone communication application with sing language support.

Prototype

Our prototype allows sign language users / deaf people to ultilize sign language to communicate online, where it would read the sign language from the webcam and translate it to either subtitle, or be sent as a normal text message, incoming voice / audio can also be translate to sign language for deafs to interpret. People who do not know sign language can also record custom hand movements as a shorthand for simple messages, these shorthands can also be shared among all users as well.
